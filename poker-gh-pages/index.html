Tiny GTO

A vectorized CFR+ solver & trainer for Kuhn poker, Leduc hold’em and Rhode Island hold’em — the same algorithm family that essentially solved heads-up limit hold’em (Cepheus, 2015). Kuhn and Leduc are solved live in your browser; Rhode Island hold’em (~871k information sets) is solved offline and shipped as a precomputed strategy, GTO-Wizard-style.
